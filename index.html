Unsupervised Regime-Based NIFTY 50 Strategy

Built an end-to-end quantitative research pipeline for NIFTY 50 equities using unsupervised learning. Engineered market and factor-based features including Garman–Klass volatility, RSI, MACD, ATR, liquidity factors, and rolling Fama–French betas. Applied K-Means clustering to identify distinct market regimes and generate systematic trading signals. Constructed portfolios using Markowitz mean–variance optimization and evaluated performance using cumulative returns, volatility, Sharpe ratio, and maximum drawdown, benchmarking results against NIFTY 50 buy-and-hold.

Time-Series Forecasting and Backtesting Engine

Designed and implemented a modular time-series research framework for financial forecasting and systematic trading. Built ARIMA and SARIMAX models on log-returns with expanding-window walk-forward evaluation to ensure causal, out-of-sample testing. Developed a fully vectorized backtesting engine incorporating transaction costs, forecast-to-signal logic, PnL attribution, Sharpe ratio, and drawdown analysis. Benchmarked model-driven strategies against naive baselines using RMSE, MAE, MAPE, and directional accuracy to assess robustness and stability across market conditions.
